OUR HISTORY

The history of Safia Cafe & Bakery is an inspiring story of a great love for baking, which turned into a real family legend and the largest confectionery brand in Uzbekistan.

It all started in 2001 with a small family workshop created by Muhaya Ayupova and her daughter Madina Ayupova. Their passion for creating sweet masterpieces quickly won the hearts of their first customers. Just six years later, Madina opened her first store called "House of Baking from Madina", where she developed her favorite business with soul and inspiration, involving the whole family in it.

In 2015, the key moment was the bold step of Madina Akmalovna's son, Bekzod Samigzhanov. He suggested turning the family business into a full-fledged brand, and thus Safia Cafe & Bakery was born. The shops have been transformed into stylish coffee shops with French charm, becoming oases of taste and comfort.
